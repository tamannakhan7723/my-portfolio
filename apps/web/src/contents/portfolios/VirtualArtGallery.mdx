## 📍 Overview

The Virtual Art Gallery simulates a real-world art exhibition in a digital format. It allows users to explore different artworks through a structured gallery layout, providing an engaging experience similar to visiting a physical gallery. The project emphasizes visual presentation, layout design, and user interaction to create an aesthetic and user-friendly interface.

### 🌟Features
- Virtual representation of an art gallery environment.  
- Display of artworks in a structured gallery layout.  
- Visually appealing and aesthetic user interface.  
- Organized presentation of images and content.  
- Smooth navigation across different sections.  
- Focus on user experience and design clarity.  
- Responsive layout for different screen sizes.  
- Interactive browsing experience.

### 📌Key Highlights
- Designed a virtual experience of a real-world art gallery.  
- Focused on UI/UX and visual storytelling.  
- Improved creativity and front-end design skills.
